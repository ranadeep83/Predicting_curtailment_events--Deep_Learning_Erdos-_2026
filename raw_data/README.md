## Raw data

This folder contains the raw data used in this project, obtained from SMARD and Open-Meteo

### SMARD
From smard.de, we have obtained hourly data from 12:00 AM, 1 May 2019 to  6:00 AM, 1 May 2026
1. "Actual_generation_201905010000_202605010600_Hour.csv"   - energy produced from various renewable and non-renewable resources
2. "Actual_consumption_201905010000_202605010600_Hour.csv"  - actual energy consumed
